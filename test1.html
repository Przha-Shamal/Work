Radia Perlman



Radia Perlman is an American engineer and mathematician, who has been called “the mother of internet” thanks to her contribution to technology development. Perlman invented the spanning tree algorithm and the spanning tree protocol (STP).

Her inventions have shaped the Internet, including:
  1. Robust
  2. Scalable
  3. Autoconfiguiring
  4. Routing Technology
